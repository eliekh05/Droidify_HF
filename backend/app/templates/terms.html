🔍 What Droidify is

A free read-only index. It fetches publicly available metadata about Android ROMs, recoveries, root tools, and devices from third-party sources and displays it in one place. It does not host, own, store, or distribute any firmware file, ROM, recovery image, or binary. Every single download link on this site goes directly to the original project's own servers. Droidify is never in the path between you and the file.

⚠️ The stuff that actually matters
🔓
Bootloader unlock wipes your device. Everything on internal storage is gone. Back up before you start. This is not optional and it is not reversible after the fact.
Every manufacturer. Every device. No exceptions.
🚫
Samsung Knox is permanent. The moment you unlock the bootloader on a Samsung device, Knox trips to 0x1. No stock flash undoes it. No Samsung service centre undoes it. Samsung Pay, Secure Folder, and Knox Workspace stop working permanently.
If any of those matter to you, do not unlock the bootloader.
📴
Play Protect certification and banking apps. Rooting or flashing an uncertified ROM can break Play Protect certification. Banking apps, streaming services, and anything that checks for device integrity may stop working.
That conversation is with the ROM developer, not us.
📜
Warranty. Unlocking the bootloader voids the manufacturer warranty on most devices. Some let you re-lock to recover it. Check your specific device and region before proceeding.
We cannot tell you whether your warranty is affected. The manufacturer can.
📷
Bricked device. Flashing the wrong file, wrong variant, or interrupting a flash can brick your device. Some bricks are recoverable. Some are not.
Go to XDA or the ROM's Telegram group. They know how to unbrick your specific device. We do not.
💾 The files we link to

Every file linked on Droidify was uploaded by its developer to their own infrastructure — LineageOS mirrors, OrangeFox CDN, TWRP's servers, GitHub Releases, SourceForge. We did not upload it. We did not verify it beyond what the source reports. If a file causes problems, the developer who uploaded it is who you need to talk to.

🐞 GitHub issues that get auto-closed

These are not things we can help with and opening an issue will not change that:

  • Tripped Knox or voided warranty from following a guide
  • Failed Play Protect certification after flashing
  • Data loss from a wipe or failed flash
  • Bricked device
  • Problems with a specific ROM or recovery file
  • Requests to recover data or unbrick a device

All of these require help from the developer who made the ROM or the community for your specific device. XDA and Telegram groups are the right place.
