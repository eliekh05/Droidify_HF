🔍 What Droidify is

A free index. Fetches publicly available data about Android ROMs, recoveries, tools, and devices from third-party sources. Does not host, own, or distribute any firmware or ROM file. Every download link goes directly to whoever made it.

⚠️ The stuff that actually matters
🔓
Bootloader unlock wipes your device. Everything. Gone. Back up first.
Not negotiable. Every manufacturer. Every device.
🚫
Samsung Knox is permanent. Unlock the bootloader = Knox trips to 0x1 forever. No stock flash, no service centre undoes it. Knox, Samsung Pay, Secure Folder stop working permanently.
If you care about any of those — do not unlock.
📴
Play Protect and banking apps. Rooting or uncertified ROMs can break certification. Banking apps and streaming services may stop working.
That conversation is with the ROM developer, not us.
📜
Warranty. Unlocking voids it on most devices. Some let you re-lock to recover it.
Check before you proceed.
📷
Bricked device. Wrong file, wrong variant, interrupted flash = permanent brick.
Go to XDA or the ROM’s Telegram. They know how to unbrick. We don’t.
💾 The files we link to

Every file was uploaded by its developer to their own infrastructure. We didn’t upload it. If a file causes problems: the developer who uploaded it is who you need.

🐞 GitHub issues — what gets auto-closed
  • Tripped Knox / voided warranty
  • Failed Play Protect certification
  • Data loss from flashing
  • Bricked device
  • Complaints about a ROM or recovery file
  • Requests to recover data or unbrick
